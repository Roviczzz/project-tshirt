The Life of an ICT Student

In my experience, being an ICT student holds a lot of potential to extend their skills at expressing and brainstorming into a project. Learning to implement is one way of working consistently, and it takes time to familiarize yourself. It helps in organizing the thought process behind the workflow.

Advices for future ICT Students

For future ICT students, Learning takes time. Starting by taking notes from others and researching for resources such as documentation and construction of a flowchart helps in working productively and efficiently. Hands-on activities are one example of creating a learning curve in any given task and are necessary to earn feedback and improvement for future projects.
